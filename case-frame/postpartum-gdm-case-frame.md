# Example case frame

## Use case
Postpartum handoff from obstetric care to primary care after gestational diabetes mellitus.

## Exchange moment
Hospital postpartum discharge.

## Sending context
Obstetric or postpartum hospital team.

## Receiving context
Primary care physician.

## Main continuity problem being addressed
Risk of losing postpartum metabolic follow-up context, interpreted risk and responsibility after discharge.

## Main risk to preserve
Postpartum and longer-term metabolic risk after gestational diabetes mellitus.

## Main follow-up to preserve
Postpartum glucose follow-up in primary care within the expected follow-up window.

## What this example is not trying to do
This example does not represent the full maternity record, the full birth episode or all maternal data.
It only represents the minimum continuity-relevant information needed for this handoff.
